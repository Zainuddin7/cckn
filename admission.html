Admission Process 2024-2025

Admission Criteria

  • Admissions open for Class 7th
  • Age limit: 11-14 years for Class 7
  • Minimum 60% marks in previous class
  • Medical fitness certificate required
  • Domicile of Balochistan preferred

Admission Procedure

  1. Registration: Submit online application at cckn.edu.pk or collect form from college office
  2. Entry Test: Appear for written test (English, Math, Urdu, General Knowledge)
  3. Interview: Shortlisted candidates will be interviewed
  4. Medical Examination: Physical fitness test at designated hospital
  5. Final Selection: Based on test (70%), interview (20%), and medical (10%)
  6. Fee Submission: Selected candidates must submit fees within 15 days
