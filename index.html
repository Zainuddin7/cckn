Welcome to Cadet College Kharan

Nestled in the heart of District Kharan. Cadet College Kharan aims to be a beacon of educational excellence and character development. Established with an aim for nurturing young minds, we take immense pride in introducing you to a place where dreams take flight, knowledge finds its roots and character is forged with unwavering determination.

A Jewel in Kharan, Balochistan

Our institution finds its home in the historic district of Kharan, where the vast Balochistan horizon meets the warm hospitality of its people. The college is not just an educational institution; it's a galaxy that represents the cadets from all over Balochistan. Here, your child will not only receive exceptional education but also experience the richness of culture and heritage, creating memories that last for lifetime.

Academic Excellence and Character Development

At Cadet College Kharan, our commitment to academic excellence is unwavering. We provide a rigorous and comprehensive educational program that nurtures the intellectual, emotional and physical growth of our cadets.

Our esteemed faculty, selected for their proficiency, competence and dedication ensures that students receive quality education. We are more than just academics, we believe in shaping well-rounded individuals.

Our college is not just a place for learning but a nurturing ground for character development. Our values of integrity, leadership and discipline are instilled in our cadets through a blend of academic, extracurricular and outdoor activities.
