Eligibility Criteria 2024-2025

Academic Requirements

  • For Class 7: Minimum 60% marks in Class 6 annual exams
  • For Class 8: Minimum 60% marks in Class 7 annual exams
  • For Class 9: Minimum 65% marks in Class 8 annual exams
  • Science subjects mandatory for Class 9 admissions
  • Original school leaving certificate required

Age Limit

Class Minimum Age Maximum Age
Class 7 11 years 13 years
Class 8 12 years 14 years
Class 9 13 years 15 years
Age calculated as on 1st April 2024. No exceptions will be made.

Physical Standards

  • Minimum height: 4'6" (137 cm)
  • Weight proportionate to height (according to BMI standards)
  • Normal vision (6/6 without glasses)
  • No chronic diseases or physical disabilities
  • Medical certificate from government hospital required

Domicile Requirements

  • First priority: Candidates from Balochistan province
  • Second priority: Sons of armed forces personnel
  • Third priority: Candidates from other provinces (limited seats)
  • Original domicile certificate must be submitted

Special Considerations

  • 5% quota for sons of martyrs
  • 3% seats reserved for special talent (sports, academics)
  • Children of college staff eligible for concession
  • No discrimination on basis of race, religion or creed

Disqualifications

  • Failed in any class more than once
  • Expelled from any educational institution
  • Involved in any criminal activity
  • Having fake or tampered documents
  • Not meeting physical standards
