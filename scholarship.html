Scholarships & Financial Aid 2024-2025

Available Scholarships

Merit-Based

Principal's Honor Scholarship

Coverage: 100% tuition fee
  • For students securing 90%+ in annual exams
  • Maintain 85%+ to continue scholarship
  • Top 3 positions in each class
  • Renewable annually
Need-Based

Kharan Talent Scholarship

Coverage: 50-75% tuition fee
  • For students from low-income families
  • Family income below Rs. 50,000/month
  • Minimum 75% marks required
  • Requires income proof documents
Special Category

Shaheed Foundation Scholarship

Coverage: 100% of all fees
  • For sons of martyred military personnel
  • No academic performance conditions
  • Requires official martyrdom certificate

Application Process

  1. Check Eligibility: Review requirements for each scholarship
  2. Collect Documents: Prepare required paperwork
  3. Submit Application: Complete scholarship form before deadline
  4. Interview: Shortlisted candidates will be interviewed
  5. Award Notification: Results announced within 30 days

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Previous year's mark sheet (attested)
  • Income certificate (for need-based)
  • Father's CNIC copy
  • Domicile certificate
  • 2 passport-sized photographs
  • Supporting documents for special categories

Scholarship Committee

All applications are reviewed by the College Scholarship Committee comprising:

  • Principal (Chairperson)
  • Vice Principal Academics
  • Vice Principal Administration
  • Senior Faculty Member
  • Finance Officer
