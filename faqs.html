Frequently Asked Questions

Admissions

What are the admission dates for 2024-2025 session?

Admissions open on 1st January 2024 and close on 31st March 2024. The entry test will be conducted on 15th April 2024, with interviews from 20th-25th April 2024.

Can students from other provinces apply?

Yes, but priority is given to Balochistan domicile holders. Only 10% of total seats are allocated for students from other provinces.

What is the age limit for Class 7 admissions?

For Class 7, candidates must be between 11-13 years old as of 1st April 2024. Age relaxation is not permitted.

Academics

Which curriculum does the college follow?

Cadet College Kharan follows the Balochistan Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education (BBISE) curriculum, ensuring our academic standards meet provincial requirements while incorporating military training and character development programs.

What are the college timings?

Academic classes run from 8:00 AM to 2:30 PM, followed by sports/military training until 5:00 PM. Boarders have supervised study hours from 7:00 PM to 9:30 PM.

Facilities

What sports facilities are available?

We have cricket, football, hockey, basketball, volleyball, and squash facilities. Indoor games like table tennis and chess are also available. The college has a swimming pool and equestrian club.

Is medical facility available on campus?

Yes, we have a fully equipped medical center with resident doctors and nursing staff. Serious cases are referred to CMH Kharan.

Boarding & Hostels

How many cadets share a room?

All cadets share rooms in groups of three, with strict separation between senior and junior cadets - seniors reside on separate floors from juniors, and intermingling between age groups in living quarters is not permitted

What items are prohibited in hostels?

Mobile phones, electronic devices, junk food, sharp objects, and any valuables are strictly prohibited. A complete list is provided at admission.

Financial Matters

What is the fee structure for 2024?

The annual fee is approximately Rs. 350,000 which includes tuition, boarding, uniform, and basic medical. Detailed breakdown is available in the Fee section.

Are there installment plans available?

Yes, fees can be paid in three installments (40% at admission, 30% in July, 30% in December) with a 5% surcharge.
